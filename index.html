Who I Am

About Me

Hi, I'm Abirami K — a Data Analyst with a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering from VIT, Vellore.

I specialize in large-scale data processing and modeling, having structured and analyzed over 100,000 records using Python and SQL to support forecasting and customer segmentation initiatives. My work includes behavioral clustering using K-Means, time-series forecasting with SARIMA and Prophet, and building analytics-ready datasets through SQL-driven transformations.

I use tools such as Tableau and Looker Studio to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support measurable, data-driven decision making. I'm driven by solving real-world problems through structured data, analytical thinking, and scalable solutions.
